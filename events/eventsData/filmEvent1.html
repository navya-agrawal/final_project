SD Asian Film Festival

Date: Dec 20 - Dec 23

Info:

San Diego is an inspiring place that accomodates many different cultures. With the influx of Asian population recently, it became important to learn about the culture and how to create an inclusive community. The Asian Film Festival, which is the largest of its kind on the North American West Coast, boasts a large collection of films produced by Asians and Asian Americans. Most screenings are funded and supported by local entities, including San Diego State University, UC San Diego, and National City Parks and Recreation. These films teach us as a community to celebrate the diverse cultures that make San Diego a cut above the rest.
