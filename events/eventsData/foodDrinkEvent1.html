The San Diego Mac and Cheese Festival

Date: Dec 16

Info:

The San Diego Mac and Cheese Fest is quickly becoming one of the most well loved events around San Diego. It is seen by locals as an event for all ages. Children, young adults, parents, even older generations can't say no to a bowl of mac! "It's like a giant pot-luck/cookoff" says Jeremy Kempton, host and creator of the festival, "with a fantastic competition between the best vendors all across San Diego." Last year's victor, Kim Shelley attributes her new restaurant's fame and success during the competition. "The secret", says Shelley "is in the barbecue chicken and the amazing environment with other local restaurants and forms of entertainment." Come learn why San Diego loves its mac and cheese!
