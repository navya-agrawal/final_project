Choose a Dancer


One of UC San Diego's famous dance teams is coming to local events near you!

This fun and educational group is here to show a side of Latino culture that every San Diegan should learn about and love. You'll wish you were dancing with them by the end of the performance!

Although they are a fairly new group, there is no reason for you not to know these talented dancers.
