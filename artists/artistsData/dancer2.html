Los Bailadores del Barrio

BIOGRAPHY:

When a group of people find out their neighbors don't even know about their culture, you can be sure they'll do something to educate the masses! Friends and neighbors from Mission Bay, Los Bailadores del Barrio, or B del B for short, gathered together to perform traditional dances in local public spaces, such as school auditoriums and even occasionally by the beach. Well, this caught everyone's attention of course, and it caught the attention of the dancers when they realized so few of the onlookers knew what traditional Mexican dancers were. Come find this group of talented dancers and discover a part of Mexico that if you did not know yet, you ought to discover by watching their performances.
