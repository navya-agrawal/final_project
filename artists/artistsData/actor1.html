The Muses 5

BIOGRAPHY:

The Muses 5 is celebrating their fourth year together as best friends and performers. Headed by Maya Angel of North Park, the troupe consists of five established women who, despite coming from all over the world, decide to now call San Diego their home. Other members include Faith Usuwaibofo of Benin City, Nigeria, Christine Kelly of Alabama, Molly Ha of Beijing China, and Zuleia Gutierrez of Tecate, Mexico. The women met at a center for English, where they attended as either students or teachers. They take several classic plays and modify them to include more particpatory women roles as well as plays based on the stories and struggles of people from different backgrounds. They are pleased to collaborate on new projects with the La Jolla Playhouse visiting local San Diego communities. See upcoming events where they will perform below.
