Nomojo

BIOGRAPHY:

Most people enjoy a little political satire these days, and most people enjoy keeping up to date with the local news as well. When Sienna Miles moved to San Diego from the Bay area up north, she realized two things: 1) SD County apparently has too many comedians by the name "Joe", and 2) we did not know what our local government was up to! So, What did she decide to do? Become comedian "Nomojo" and tell us what weren't hearing on the news! Thanks to her recent work, we are now constantly up to date with the latest local news, and we're learning it in an entertaining way! Check out when you can see her live!
